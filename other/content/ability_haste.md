# Ability Haste
https://predecessor.wiki.gg/wiki/Ability_Haste

## Overview

Ability Haste is the stat which reduces the Cooldown of Abilities in Predecessor. Every ability in the game is affected by this mechanic, with the exception of Crest active abilities, most passives, Blink, and Ward/Sentry usage.

## Cooldown Reduction Formula

The percentage reduction in ability cooldowns is calculated using:

```
Cooldown Reduction % = 100 Ã— Ability Haste / (100 + Ability Haste)
```

Note: Ability Haste has diminishing returns past 50% Cooldown Reduction.

## Ultimate Haste

Ultimate Haste is a type of Ability Haste that only affects your Ultimate ability. It stacks additively with Ability Haste.
