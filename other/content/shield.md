# Shield
https://predecessor.wiki.gg/wiki/Shield

## Overview

Shields are an addition of hit-points that absorb damage in place of actual Health. They can persist based on two factors: shield strength (damage absorption capacity) and shield duration (time limit). Notably, shields benefit from Armor statistics.

## Classification

Shield is categorized as both a:
- **Gameplay element**
- **Statistic** (under the Defensive category)
