# Lifesteal
https://predecessor.wiki.gg/wiki/Lifesteal

## Lifesteal

Lifesteal is the stat which determines how much Health you will regain upon successfully landing a Basic Attack on an enemy.

Key points:
- Only affects Basic Attacks, not Abilities
- Available to all heroes
- Most beneficial for heroes with high Physical scaling
- Acquired only in percentage amounts

### Items with Lifesteal

- Crimson Edge
- Eviscerator
- Exodus
- Liberator
- Manta Scythe
- Marksman Crest
- Onixian Quiver
- Pacifier
- Razorclaw
- Sharpshooter Crest
- Sky Splitter
- Syonic Echo
- Terminus

## Magical Lifesteal

Magical Lifesteal is the stat which determines how much Health you will regain upon successfully landing an Ability on an enemy.

Despite its name, this applies to any Ability damage output, whether Physical or Magical. Like standard lifesteal, it's only acquired in percentage amounts.

### Items with Magical Lifesteal

- Blood Tome
- Lifebinder
- Overseer

## Omnivamp

Omnivamp benefits all damage types (Magical, Physical, True) at reduced scaling. Cleave damage does not benefit from this stat.

### Items with Omnivamp

- Bloodletter
- Brutallax
- Champion Crest
- Gravitum
- Iceskorn Talons
- Judgement
- Lucky Feather
- Magnify
- Mutilator
- Nightfall
- Rapture
- Warrior Crest

## Additional Notes

- Lifesteal calculations use damage after mitigation
- Damage-over-Time effects restore 100% of lifesteal value
- Area-of-Effect damage has a 0.33 multiplier for lifesteal gains
