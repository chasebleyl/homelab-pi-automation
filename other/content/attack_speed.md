# Attack Speed
https://predecessor.wiki.gg/wiki/Attack_Speed

## Definition

Attack speed is the rate at which your Hero can use their Basic Attacks. The mechanic includes a hard cap of 3 attacks per second, typically requiring around 350-420% Attack speed for most heroes to reach this maximum.

## Key Information

- **Hard Cap**: 3 attacks per second
- **Typical Threshold**: 350-420% Attack speed for most heroes
- **Display**: Your attack timer appears as the fourth widget on your Ability Bar
- **Wasted Stats**: Building beyond the hard cap provides no additional benefit

## Special Hero Effects

Certain champions have unique interactions with this statistic:

- **Crunch**: Ability Cast Times decrease as Attack Speed increases
- **Revenant**: Hellfire Round reload time becomes shorter with additional Attack Speed
