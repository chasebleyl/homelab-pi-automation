# Cooldown
https://predecessor.wiki.gg/wiki/Cooldown

## Definition

A Cooldown is the time period you must wait in between certain actions.

## Affected Actions

Cooldowns apply to:
- Abilities
- Blink
- Items
- Stealth Ward
- Other vision tools

## Ability Haste Interaction

All Abilities in Predecessor are impacted by the Ability Haste stat, which reduces cooldown durations proportionally based on how much of this statistic a given Hero possesses.

## Related Statistics

Cooldown falls under the Utility statistics category, alongside:
- Ability Haste
- Movement Speed
