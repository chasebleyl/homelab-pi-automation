# Attack Range
https://predecessor.wiki.gg/wiki/Attack_Range

## Overview

Attack Range is the range of your basic attacks. It is 1300 for all ranged heroes, and 260-330 (based on hitbox) range for melee heroes.

## Key Information

- **Ranged Heroes**: 1300 attack range
- **Melee Heroes**: 260-330 attack range (varies by character hitbox size)

## Sources of Increase

Some abilities and items can increase a hero's attack range. An example is Murdock's ability "Shots Fired," which provides this enhancement.

## Related Statistics

Attack Range falls under the Damage category of character statistics, alongside:
- Power
- Penetration
- Attack Speed
- Critical Strike Chance
- Lifesteal
