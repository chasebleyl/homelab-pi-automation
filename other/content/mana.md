# Mana
https://predecessor.wiki.gg/wiki/Mana

## Overview

Mana is a resource that all heroes use to fuel their abilities. Each hero possesses a distinct mana pool that grows as they level up. When heroes cast abilities, the mana cost is subtracted from their available pool. Heroes can restore mana through various game mechanics referred to as Mana Regen.
