# Heal & Shield Power
https://predecessor.wiki.gg/wiki/Heal_%26_Shield_Power

## Overview

Heal & Shield Power increases the value of Healing and Shielding that the player outputs, regardless of whether they are applied to themselves or their Allies. This statistic does not affect Health Regen.

## Formula

The calculation for Heal & Shield Power follows this structure:

```
((Scaling% × Magical Power + Base Healing) × (1 + HSP))
```

### Example Calculation

```
((60% × 120) + 200) × (1 + 10%) = 299 (rounded down)
```

## Items Providing Heal & Shield Power

The following items grant this statistic:

- Crystal Tear
- Devotion
- Divine Pouch
- Divine Wreath
- Everbloom
- Lifecore
- Lunaria
- Transference
- Windcaller

## Classification

Heal & Shield Power is categorized as a defensive statistic within the game's broader statistics system, which also includes armor, health, mana, tenacity, and shield values.
